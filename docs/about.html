About Solitaire Games HQ

Solitaire Games HQ is a free casual card game site built for players who want quick, clean solitaire games in the browser. You can play classics like Klondike, Freecell, Spider, Pyramid, Golf, Yukon, Forty Thieves, and several less common solitaire variants without downloading software or creating an account.

Our goal is simple: keep the games easy to start, readable on modern screens, and supported by helpful game guides that explain the rules in plain English. Some solitaire sites bury the actual game under pop-ups and confusing buttons. We aim for a calmer setup: choose a game, deal the cards, and play.

This website is for casual entertainment only. The games do not involve real-money gambling, wagering, cash prizes, financial rewards, or casino-style payouts. Wins and losses are just part of the card puzzle.
