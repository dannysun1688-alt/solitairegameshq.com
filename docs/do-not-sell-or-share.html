Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Effective date: July 2, 2026

This California privacy page explains how Solitaire Games HQ handles requests under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act, commonly referred to as the CCPA. It is intended for California residents and supplements our Privacy Policy.

Solitaire Games HQ is a free casual card game website. You can play without creating an account, and the site does not offer real-money gambling, wagering, cash prizes, financial rewards, or casino payouts.

Our Do Not Sell or Share Statement

Solitaire Games HQ does not sell personal information for money. We also do not knowingly sell or share personal information of users under 16 years old.

The site may use advertising, analytics, cookies, local storage, and similar technologies. Some California privacy rules may treat certain advertising or cross-context behavioral advertising activities as a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. You may use the methods below to opt out of any sale or sharing that may occur through advertising or analytics technology.

How to Opt Out

To submit a request to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information, email us at admin@solitairegameshq.com with the subject line "CCPA Opt-Out Request". You may include the browser and device you use to visit the site so we can better understand your request.

You can also reduce personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using industry opt-out tools such as aboutads.info. Your browser may also let you block or delete cookies and clear local storage.

Where required by law, we will treat a recognized browser-based opt-out preference signal, such as Global Privacy Control, as a request to opt out of sale or sharing for that browser.

California CCPA Rights

Subject to legal limits and verification requirements, California residents may have the following rights:

  • Right to know: You may request information about the categories of personal information we collect, the sources of that information, the purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties to whom information may be disclosed.
  • Right to access: You may request a copy of specific personal information we maintain about you, if any.
  • Right to delete: You may request deletion of personal information we collected from you, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Right to correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
  • Right to opt out: You may direct us not to sell or share your personal information.
  • Right to limit sensitive personal information: We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information for the purpose of inferring characteristics about you.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

Personal Information We May Collect

Because this is a no-account browser game site, the information we collect is limited. Depending on how you use the site, we may collect or receive:

  • Device and browser information, such as browser type, operating system, screen size, and general device type.
  • Internet activity information, such as pages viewed, game pages visited, referring URLs, and general site usage activity.
  • Approximate location inferred from IP address, such as country, state, or city-level region.
  • Advertising and analytics identifiers, cookies, or similar technologies used to measure performance, prevent abuse, and serve ads.
  • Local game data stored on your device, such as settings, game state, and statistics.
  • Contact information you voluntarily provide if you email us, such as your email address and message content.

Why We Use Information

We use information to operate the games, keep the site secure, measure performance, fix bugs, understand how visitors use the site, respond to messages, comply with legal obligations, and support advertising that helps keep the games free.

Data Selling Prohibition Rules

We do not exchange your personal information for money. We do not maintain a user account database for gameplay. We do not knowingly sell or share personal information from children under 16. If we receive a valid opt-out request, we will honor it as required by the CCPA and will not knowingly sell or share the personal information covered by that request unless you later authorize it or the law permits otherwise.

How to Make Other CCPA Requests

To exercise your rights to know, access, delete, correct, or opt out, email admin@solitairegameshq.com. Please use a clear subject line such as "CCPA Request", "CCPA Delete Request", or "CCPA Opt-Out Request". We may need to verify your request before responding, especially if you ask us to access, delete, or correct information.

You may use an authorized agent to submit a request if the agent provides proof of authorization and we can verify the request as required by law.
